# SaaS with Generous Free Tiers

> Services and platforms that offer significant value for free, perfect for hobbyists, startups, and developers.

## ☁️ Cloud & Hosting

- [Vercel](https://vercel.com/) - Develop, preview, and ship. Best for frontend frameworks like Next.js. (🌗 Freemium | ☁️ SaaS)
- [Netlify](https://www.netlify.com/) - The fastest way to build the fastest sites. Static hosting and serverless functions. (🌗 Freemium | ☁️ SaaS)
- [Render](https://render.com/) - Unified cloud to build and run all your apps and websites with free SSL, global CDN, and DDoS protection. (🌗 Freemium | ☁️ SaaS)
- [Fly.io](https://fly.io/) - Run your full stack apps (and databases) close to your users. (🌗 Freemium | ☁️ SaaS)
- [Cloudflare Pages](https://pages.cloudflare.com/) - JAMstack platform for frontend developers to collaborate and deploy websites. (🆓 Free | ☁️ SaaS)

## 🗄️ Database (DBaaS)

- [Supabase](https://supabase.com/) - The open source Firebase alternative. Postgres database, Authentication, instant APIs, Realtime subscriptions, and Storage. (🌗 Freemium | 🐧 Open Source)
- [Neon](https://neon.tech/) - Serverless Postgres. Separate storage and compute to scale down to zero. (🌗 Freemium | ☁️ SaaS)
- [PlanetScale](https://planetscale.com/) - The MySQL-compatible serverless database platform. (🌗 Freemium | ☁️ SaaS)
- [MongoDB Atlas](https://www.mongodb.com/atlas/database) - The multi-cloud developer data platform. (🌗 Freemium | ☁️ SaaS)
- [Redis Cloud](https://redis.com/try-free/) - Fully managed Redis service. (🌗 Freemium | ☁️ SaaS)

## 🔑 Authentication & User Management

- [Clerk](https://clerk.com/) - Complete user management and authentication. (🌗 Freemium | ☁️ SaaS)
- [Auth0](https://auth0.com/) - Secure access for everyone. But not just anyone. (🌗 Freemium | ☁️ SaaS)
- [Firebase Auth](https://firebase.google.com/products/auth) - Backend services for using authentication supporting passwords, phone numbers and popular federated identity providers. (🆓 Free | ☁️ SaaS)

## 📧 Email & Communication

- [Resend](https://resend.com/) - Email for developers. The best API to reach humans instead of spam folders. (🌗 Freemium | ☁️ SaaS)
- [SendGrid](https://sendgrid.com/) - Cloud-based email delivery platform. (🌗 Freemium | ☁️ SaaS)
- [Postmark](https://postmarkapp.com/) - Email delivery for web apps. (🌗 Freemium | ☁️ SaaS)
- [Mailgun](https://www.mailgun.com/) - Email API service for developers. (🌗 Freemium | ☁️ SaaS)

## 🔍 Monitoring & Logging

- [Sentry](https://sentry.io/) - Application performance monitoring and error tracking software. (🌗 Freemium | 🐧 Open Source)
- [Better Stack](https://betterstack.com/) - Collaborative observability platform. Uptime monitoring, incident management, and status pages. (🌗 Freemium | ☁️ SaaS)
- [Logtail](https://betterstack.com/logtail) - SQL-compatible log management. (🌗 Freemium | ☁️ SaaS)

## 🖼️ Media & Image Processing

- [Cloudinary](https://cloudinary.com/) - Image and video management in the cloud. (🌗 Freemium | ☁️ SaaS)
- [Shots](https://shots.so/) - Quick mockups, animated templates and device previews. (🆓 Free | ☁️ SaaS)
- [Squoosh](https://squoosh.app/) - Image compression and optimization tool that runs locally in the browser. (🆓 Free | 🐧 Open Source)
- [UploadThing](https://uploadthing.com/) - File uploads for modern web apps. (🌗 Freemium | 🐧 Open Source)
